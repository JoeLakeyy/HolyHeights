Holy Heights

100 of the tallest religious buildings in history

Explore the height of religious buildings throughout history from the Great Pyramid of Giza to the Sagrada Família.

Navigation:
• Move the timeline slider through the centuries to see when each structure was begun, completed, or destroyed.
• ⏮ Reset the slider
• ▶/⏸ Play/pause timeline
• 1x-8x Adjust playback speed
• ◀/▶ Step backwards or forwards one year
• Use the filters to focus on specific continents or religions.
• +/- zoom level
• Click any marker on the map to view detailed information.
• Refer to the legend for icon meanings and build/end/destruction timeline colours.

Inclusion criteria: Buildings must either be a place of worship, statue representing a religious figure, a burial of religious significance, or housing for a religious figure. The single highest point is taken for each building. The 100 buildings selected are not strictly the tallest ever constructed; Religious significance, historical context and editorial judgement guided the selection.

Technical stuff: Height over time is modelled as a linear progression between construction start and construction end. This dataset has been constructed primarily from wikipedia pages, and where crucial information was missing or ambiguous other sources were used. Copliot assisted in this project by refining parts of the syntax and data structure.
